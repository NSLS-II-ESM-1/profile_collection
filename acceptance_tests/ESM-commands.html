Common commands:

1. move from branch A to B

  RE(Beamline.move_to('Branch_A')) 

2. M3 pitch allignment

  RE(M3_pitch_alignment('A'))

3. Change photon energy

  RE(Eph.move_to(90, grating='600', EPU='105', shutter='open'))

4. Move Low-T manip:

RE(LT_manip.move_to('Analysis'))                           
RE(LT_manip.move_to('Low_Temp'))     

5. RE.clear_suspenders()
